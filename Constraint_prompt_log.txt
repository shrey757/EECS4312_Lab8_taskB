Constraint Prompt 1:
I am working on Lab 9 for an Event Registration with Waitlist system. I am still at the persona interpretation stage and do NOT want code. Please help me understand the two personas and extract the main user characteristics that could affect system behavior.

Persona 1: Jane Adams (Primary User)
- Busy professional managing routines and commitments
- Relies on automation to reduce cognitive load
- Acts quickly and rarely revisits system suggestions
- Trusts the system to resolve conflicts automatically
- Prefers concise outputs and minimal interaction
- Needs predictable and stable ordering of system decisions
- Needs automatic handling of conflicts
- Needs limited notification or information overload
- Pain points: too many notifications, inconsistent behavior, manual conflict resolution

Persona 2: Mo Rodriguez (Edge User)
- User with accessibility needs and sensitivity to uncertainty
- Carefully reviews outputs and seeks explanations
- Performs frequent updates such as cancellations
- Experiences stress when outcomes appear arbitrary
- Needs deterministic behavior under ties/conflicts
- Needs clear explanations when promotions or rejections occur
- Needs explicit handling of edge cases instead of silent failure
- Pain points: not understanding why the system changed, silent failures, inconsistent responses

Please do the following:
1. Summarize the key characteristics of each persona.
2. Identify which persona attributes are most likely to affect system behavior.
3. Do not generate code.

Response summary:
The LLM summarized Jane as a user who values predictability, automation, and concise interaction, and Mo as a user who values determinism, explanations, and explicit edge-case handling. It identified ordering, automatic promotion, concise outputs, duplicate-message avoidance, deterministic behavior, and explanation support as key behavior-affecting attributes.

Decision:
Accepted. Used this response to write the Persona Interpretation section in Form 1.
